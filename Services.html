Our Services

Furniture Re-upholstering
Transform and recreate your living spaces with our re-upholstering services. We provide a variety of options for soft finishes, including fabric choices, window treatments, headboards, ottomans, chairs, sofas, and more.

Custom-Made Furnishings
Our bespoke services ensure that your space becomes a personal escape. We design and create custom furniture tailored to your specific requirements and lifestyle, including sofas, chairs, tables, TV units, home theaters, and other furnishings.

Hard Finishes Design
We specialize in selecting and designing all your hard finishes, such as tile layouts, tile choices, ceiling designs, bathroom layouts, sanitary ware, and lighting designs, to ensure a cohesive and stylish environment.

Specifying and Sourcing
We take care of specifying and sourcing any items you require, from cutlery and crockery to linen and more, ensuring every detail aligns with your vision.

Handpicked Accessories
To achieve the perfect finish, we handpick accessories, including antiques, rugs, art, and framing, to complement and enhance your space.

Our Promise
No matter your style or budget, we embark on a transformative journey with your ideal space in mind, ensuring a seamless blend of functionality and aesthetics.

At Funa Style Concepts, we are committed to making your space a true reflection of your taste and lifestyle. Let us help you create a home that is not only beautiful but also uniquely yours.
